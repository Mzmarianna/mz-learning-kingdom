### **Total Badges Needed: 48**

### **Design Philosophy for All Badges:**
- **Shape:** Circular or shield-shaped (friendly, not intimidating)
- **Size:** 512x512px minimum (scalable)
- **Style:** Cute, playful, 3D-looking with soft shadows
- **Colors:** Match neurodivergent-first palette (cyan, purple, pink, green, gold, amber - NO RED)
- **Elements:** Include sparkles, stars, shine effects, glows
- **Typography:** If text included, use friendly rounded fonts
- **Emotion:** Celebratory, encouraging, never judgmental
- **File Format:** PNG with transparency OR SVG for infinite scaling
