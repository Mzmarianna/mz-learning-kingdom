- Patent AI tutoring methods

### **Risk 2: Tutor Quality/Supply**
**Scenario**: Can't hire enough good tutors to scale

**Mitigation:**
- AI augmentation (Wowl does 80% of tutoring)
- Tutor training certification program
- Hire neurodivergent tutors (lived experience)
- Competitive pay + equity for early tutors
- Franchise model (local operators find tutors)

---

### **Risk 3: ESA Funding Disappears**
**Scenario**: Political changes eliminate ESA programs

**Mitigation:**
- Diversify to private pay (build brand)
- School district contracts (B2B SaaS)
- International markets (Canada, UK, Australia)
- Scholarships (philanthropic funding)
- Freemium model (free tier with ads)

---

### **Risk 4: Regulatory Changes**
**Scenario**: COPPA/FERPA becomes more strict, or AI regulation

**Mitigation:**
- Hire compliance team early
- Privacy-first architecture (no data selling)
- Parental consent flows
- Transparent AI (explainable decisions)
- Lobbying (join SIIA, CoSN)

---

### **Risk 5: Curriculum Development Too Slow**
**Scenario**: Takes 3 years to build L1-L6 (kills growth)

**Mitigation:**
- Launch with L1 only (6 months)
- Repurpose open educational resources (OER)
- AI-generated first drafts (GPT-4)
- Hire curriculum team (3-5 people)
- Partner with existing curriculum providers
